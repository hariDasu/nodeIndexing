Offering Chicago's only ALA-accredited Master of Library and Information Science, Dominican University is a national leader in library and information science, where students work side-by-side with professors who bring years of experience to the classroom.

Our expertise in library science, information management, archival and cultural heritage resources, and school library media will help prepare you to join our hundreds of alumni working in libraries, schools, archives, museums and corporations to help people connect with the world around them.
